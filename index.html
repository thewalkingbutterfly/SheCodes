Gillies is a store founded by Angelika Berger.

Angelika Berger is a cancer survivor who lives in the beautiful, historic city of Marburg , a German town north of Frankfurt. In 2011, at 52 years old, she was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer and in between chemo treatments she started a new hobby: the art of crafting jewellery. And this hobby became a passion. A passion which now she desires to share with people all around the world. Read more about Angelika's story here.
